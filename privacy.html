Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how What's for Dinner? ("we", "our", or "the site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from visitors unless you voluntarily submit our contact form. When you submit the partnership inquiry form, we collect:

  • Your email address

This information is transmitted securely to the site owner via Formspree and is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. Cookies

This website uses cookies to improve user experience. Cookies are small data files stored in your browser. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Preferences: We store your chosen language and color theme (light/dark mode) in your browser's local storage so your settings are remembered on future visits.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense may place cookies on your device to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests and browsing history.
  • Comments: Disqus may place cookies to enable the comment section and remember your login state.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this site.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites across the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Disqus Comments

The comments section on this site is powered by Disqus. When you interact with the Disqus widget, Disqus may collect information including your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. This information is governed by Disqus's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

This site relies on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense / Google Privacy Policy

  • Formspree Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Contact form submissions are handled entirely by Formspree and subject to their data retention policies.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out using the contact form on our main page.
